My Favorite Super Heroes

Batman

Batman uses his intellect, his fighting skills and vast amount of money to fight crime. His secret identity is Bruce Wayne.

The Flash

The Flash uses his speed to combat evildoers. Many have taken the mantle of the speedster but the first and best is Barry Allen

Wolverine

Wolverine uses his adamantium claws, agility, and healing factor to rip apart anyone who stands in his way
